## Cyrill Stachniss

[Website](https://www.ipb.uni-bonn.de/people/cyrill-stachniss/)

I worked with Prof. Cyrill Stachniss during my research internship at the Lab for Photogrammetry and Robotics at the University of Bonn. Our collaboration focused on adaptive informative path planning in unknown three-dimensional environments using dynamic graph representations and deep reinforcement learning.

### Selected collaboration

- **Deep Reinforcement Learning with Dynamic Graphs for Adaptive Informative Path Planning**

### Research areas

- Mobile robotics
- Autonomous navigation
- Mapping and perception
- Informative path planning
