Professor Aniket Bera is my Ph.D. advisor at Purdue University. Our work focuses on embodied AI, robot planning, vision-language-action models, reinforcement learning, and intelligent multi-robot systems.

### Selected collaborations

- **CoReLIN:** Constraint-based reasoning for lifelong interactive navigation
- **Scalable Multi-Robot Informative Path Planning:** Deep reinforcement learning for target mapping in unknown environments
