## Guillaume Sartoretti

[MARMoT Laboratory](https://www.marmotlab.org/)

I worked with Prof. Guillaume Sartoretti during my research internship at the Multi-Agent Robotic Motion Laboratory at the National University of Singapore. Our collaborations have focused on learning-based informative path planning, multi-agent robotics, and reinforcement learning for autonomous systems.

### Selected collaborations

- **CAtNIPP:** Context-aware attention-based learning for informative path planning
- **CoReLIN:** Constraint-based reasoning for zero-shot lifelong interactive navigation

### Research areas

- Multi-agent robotics
- Informative path planning
- Reinforcement learning
- Robot navigation and coordination
